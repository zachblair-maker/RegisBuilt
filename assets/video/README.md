# Hero background video

Drop your Higgsfield render here as **`hero.mp4`** (and optionally `hero.webm`).
Until then the hero shows the poster still (`assets/img/projects/kennards-truganina.jpg`).

Recommended specs:
- 1920×1080 (16:9), H.264 MP4, ~6–12s **seamless loop**, no audio.
- Keep it ~5–15 MB (compress; it autoplays muted on every visit).
- Slow, subtle motion — a gentle drone push/aerial or interior dolly reads best under the dark scrim + headline.
