The people behind the build

Experienced hands, one accountable team.

Storage is a long-horizon, high-value undertaking. It deserves a team that has done it many times before — and stays with you from the first conversation to handover.

Managing Director & Founder

Anthony Regis

Anthony began his career in structural and civil engineering in 1992, working on the engineering design of towers across Melbourne — work recognised with a President's Award for Engineering Excellence for his contribution to the Clarendon Towers. He entered the self storage industry in 2000 and founded RegisBuilt in 2003. A registered commercial builder in both Australia and New Zealand, and a major shareholder in two Melbourne storage facilities, Anthony brings a rare blend of engineering rigour, construction expertise and commercial insight — evident across feasibility, design, estimating, cost planning and project delivery.

Project Development Manager

Ken Snow

Ken began his working life as a fitter and turner, adding qualifications in industrial preservation and fluid power technology that led to technical roles across a range of companies. That grounding served him well when he moved into technical sales in 1998 with a company supplying lifts to the self storage industry, managing key projects nationally. With more than three decades in construction and self storage, Ken is recognised for delivering large-scale projects to the highest standard — involved from the first client conversation through to handover.
