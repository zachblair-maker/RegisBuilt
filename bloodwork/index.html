Important — please read

  • This tool does not provide medical advice. It is an educational, harm-reduction resource. It cannot diagnose, treat, or replace a licensed physician, endocrinologist, or laboratory.
  • Reference ranges vary between labs, assays, age, and individuals. Always interpret your results using the ranges printed on your own lab report and with a clinician who knows your history.
  • Supplement and lifestyle suggestions are general education, not prescriptions. They can interact with medications and conditions. Talk to a doctor or pharmacist before starting anything.
  • Some values are emergencies. Very high hematocrit, blood pressure, liver enzymes, or potassium — or symptoms like chest pain, severe headache, shortness of breath, or fainting — mean you should seek urgent medical care now, not read a website.
  • The safest choice is always to use these substances under medical supervision, or not at all. This tool exists because people use them anyway and deserve information that keeps them safer.
