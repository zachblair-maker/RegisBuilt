Overview

In July 2022, RegisBuilt was awarded the design and construction contract for Kennards Self Storage at 940 Thompsons Road, Cranbourne West. Works commenced on site for Stage 1 in November 2022.

The staged delivery approach allows capacity to be brought online progressively — managed end to end by RegisBuilt across design, approvals, structure and fitout.

Scope of works

  • Engineering & architectural design
  • Town planning & approvals
  • Staged construction delivery
  • Cladding, roller doors & fitout
  • Handover
