Overview

In December 2024, RegisBuilt was awarded the design and construction contract for the StorHub conversion project at North Lakes, Queensland — transforming an existing building into a modern self storage facility.

Conversions play to RegisBuilt’s strengths: working alongside other contractors on foundational works where needed, while delivering the external structure, cladding and complete fitout.
