Overview

RegisBuilt was awarded the design and construction contract for Kennards Self Storage at 332 Leakes Road, Truganina in June 2019. Located in Melbourne’s western suburbs, the completed facility was handed over in July 2020.

Delivered in stages, Stage 1 comprised two separate buildings totalling around 7,200 sqm GFA and 500 storage spaces — with the facility planned to roughly double to about 13,000 sqm and more than 750 units at full build-out.

Delivered to Kennards’ national operating standard, the project drew on RegisBuilt’s full design-and-construct capability — from engineering and approvals through to structure, cladding, roller doors and fitout.

Scope of works

  • Engineering & architectural design
  • Town planning & approvals
  • Construction & structure
  • Cladding, roller doors & fitout
  • Handover
