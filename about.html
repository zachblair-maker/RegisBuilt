Why RegisBuilt

A builder that thinks like an engineer.

RegisBuilt was founded in 2003 on a foundation of structural and civil engineering. That heritage runs through everything we do — facilities are designed to be robust, efficient to run and built to last, not simply put up.

Australian owned and a registered commercial builder in both Australia and New Zealand, we deliver everything a facility owner needs from a single source — from feasibility and design through to construction. The result is a partner you can trust with high-value, long-horizon projects.

  • A trusted partner. The highest-quality steel storage systems, for facilities that stay close to maintenance-free for years.
  • Genuine industry experience. Two decades of insight into what facility owners actually need, from design through to erection.
  • Quality and value, balanced. Built right the first time — avoiding the long-run cost of inferior materials and workmanship.
