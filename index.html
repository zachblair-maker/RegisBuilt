Capabilities

Everything a storage build demands, under one roof.

From the first feasibility study to the final fitout, RegisBuilt covers every discipline a storage project requires — engineering, design and construction — from a single, accountable source.

01

Consulting & Feasibility

Site selection, feasibility studies and finance guidance — so the commercial case is sound long before construction begins.

02

Planning & Approvals

Town planning, permits and authority coordination, managed in-house to keep the program moving and the timeline intact.

03

Engineering & Design

Layouts engineered to maximise rentable area and unit mix — drawing on a structural and civil engineering foundation.

04

Design & Construction

Turnkey delivery of purpose-built facilities — structure, cladding, doors and fitout — built to operator-grade standards.

05

Build Overs & Multi-Storey

Complex multi-level structures and build-overs — partnering on foundations while we deliver the external structure and fitout above.

06

Conversions & Fitouts

Transforming existing warehouses into high-performing storage, with quality fitouts delivered to sites nationally.

Why RegisBuilt

A builder that thinks like an engineer.

RegisBuilt was founded in 2003 on a foundation of structural and civil engineering. That heritage runs through everything we do — facilities are designed to be robust, efficient to run and built to last, not simply put up.

Australian owned and a registered commercial builder in both Australia and New Zealand, we deliver everything a facility owner needs from a single source — from feasibility and design through to construction. The result is a partner you can trust with high-value, long-horizon projects.

  • A trusted partner. The highest-quality steel storage systems, for facilities that stay close to maintenance-free for years.
  • Genuine industry experience. Two decades of insight into what facility owners actually need, from design through to erection.
  • Quality and value, balanced. Built right the first time — avoiding the long-run cost of inferior materials and workmanship.
