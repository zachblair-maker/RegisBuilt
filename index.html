How we work

A considered process, start to finish.

Every project is led by a single point of contact with experience across all five stages — so accountability never changes hands.

Stage 01 / 05

Consult

We start with the numbers. The site, the local market, demand and the commercial case — including indicative yields and unit mix — are assessed so you know the opportunity stacks up before a dollar is committed.

  • Feasibility & yield analysis
  • Market & demand assessment
  • Finance and site guidance
Stage 02 / 05

Plan

Town planning, permits and authority approvals — coordinated and tracked in-house. We manage the statutory pathway end to end to protect your program and keep momentum from day one.

  • Town planning & permits
  • Authority coordination
  • Program & risk tracking
Stage 03 / 05

Design

Layouts engineered to maximise rentable area and optimise unit mix, with structure, access and security designed around how the facility will actually operate — and how it will be built.

  • Rentable-area optimisation
  • Unit-mix & access design
  • Engineered to build
Stage 04 / 05

Build

Construction managed tightly to program and budget by a single accountable team, with quality control at every stage and one point of contact who has been across the project since the first conversation.

  • Single accountable team
  • Program & budget control
  • Quality control at every stage
Stage 05 / 05

Handover

A finished, fitted-out facility delivered ready to trade — commissioned, compliant and operating — with the support to back it long after the keys change hands.

  • Commissioned & compliant
  • Fitted out, ready to trade
  • Ongoing support

Why RegisBuilt

A builder that thinks like an engineer.

RegisBuilt was founded in 2003 on a foundation of structural and civil engineering. That heritage runs through everything we do — facilities are designed to be robust, efficient to run and built to last, not simply put up.

Australian owned and a registered commercial builder in both Australia and New Zealand, we deliver everything a facility owner needs from a single source — from feasibility and design through to construction. The result is a partner you can trust with high-value, long-horizon projects.

  • A trusted partner. The highest-quality steel storage systems, for facilities that stay close to maintenance-free for years.
  • Genuine industry experience. Two decades of insight into what facility owners actually need, from design through to erection.
  • Quality and value, balanced. Built right the first time — avoiding the long-run cost of inferior materials and workmanship.
